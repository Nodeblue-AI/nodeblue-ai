## About Nodeblue

Nodeblue is an independent applied research lab studying how software can read, reason over, and act inside the real operations the physical world runs on. A generation of software learned to *measure* the world; we study what it takes for a machine to *reason about* it and act inside it — alongside the people doing the work.

Our research produces working systems. The first, **Nexus**, already runs on live plant floors.

We are a small group of researchers and engineers with backgrounds at **Tesla**, **Amazon**, **Applied Materials**, and beyond — teams that have shipped production-critical systems where failure isn't an option.

**Founded:** 2023

**Headquarters:** St. Petersburg, Florida, USA

---

## What We Are Exploring

One idea, explored from every angle: most AI never touches the systems a business actually runs on. We study what it takes to change that.

**Operational Intelligence** — Teaching systems to read a live operation the way an experienced engineer does, then reason over what they see. *Running today in Nexus.*

**Knowledge & Memory** — How an organization retains what it learns and makes it usable long after the people who learned it have moved on. *In active prototyping as Atlas.*

**Human & AI Collaboration** — Designing the division of labor between human judgment and machine reasoning so each does what it is good at. *Built into Nexus today.*

**Autonomy & Execution** — What it takes for a system to cross from understanding a situation to safely acting inside it. *The furthest edge, becoming Forge.*

## Systems

Each system takes a piece of the research into a real operating environment.

### [Nexus](https://www.nodeblue.ai/nexus) · `in use`

Operational intelligence for industrial environments. Nexus reads a plant's control logic, supervisory systems, and documentation together, and reasons over them to diagnose faults and answer questions in plain language — cited to the exact source. It is the first place our research meets a working floor.

→ [nexus.nodeblue.ai](https://nexus.nodeblue.ai)

### [Atlas](https://www.nodeblue.ai/systems) · `in development`

A living model of how an operation works. Research into a system that maps how an organization actually runs, so its knowledge stays current instead of decaying in documents.

### [Forge](https://www.nodeblue.ai/systems) · `in development`

From understanding an operation to acting in it. Research into what lets a system cross from analysis to safe action, with the verification and accountability real consequences demand.

---

## How We Work

**Inside the loop, not above it.** We study what it takes for a system to read a live operation, reason over it, and act on it safely — then prove it against real systems rather than synthetic demos. Nexus is where that research runs today, on a live floor. The rest of the work is how we get there.
